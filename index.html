MUASI is a collection that aims to sensitize people against violence against women, such as rape, domestic violence and others... The rapes that we know, is a subject very present especially in the east of the Democratic Republic of Congo where the war has been raging for a long time. The rate of domestic violence, as we know, continues to increase every year. The goal of MUASI is to be able to come and provide support to these suffering people, by providing them even love, material support for those wishing to rebuild but also financial support.
